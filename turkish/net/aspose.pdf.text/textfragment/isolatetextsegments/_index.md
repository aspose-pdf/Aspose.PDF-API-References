## TextFragment.IsolateTextSegments metodu

```csharp
public TextSegmentCollection IsolateTextSegments(int startIndex, int length)
```

| Parametre | Tür | Açıklama |
| --- | --- | --- |
| startIndex | Int32 | Yeni [`TextSegment`](../../textsegment/)(lerinin başlayacağı metindeki konum. |
